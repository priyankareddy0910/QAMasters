Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know before joining.

Will I get recordings for the sessions? +
Yes! All class recordings and notes are uploaded to Google Drive daily. You have lifetime access to all materials even after the batch ends.
Is this useful for freshers with no coding experience? +
Absolutely! All concepts are explained from scratch. No prior programming or automation experience is required to join this course.
What if I miss a class? +
No worries! You will get the recording for each session the same day. You can watch and catch up at your own pace. Lifetime access ensures you never fall behind.
How does the mock interview work? +
Every week there will be a mock interview session simulating real interview scenarios. This includes scenario-based questions, framework design discussions, and live coding — so you're fully prepared before the real thing.
When do I pay the course fee? +
The first 3 classes are completely free. After attending 3 classes, if you wish to continue, you can enroll by paying the course fee of ₹11,000 (India) or $130 (USA/International). No pressure — decide after experiencing the quality yourself!
Will I get support after the batch for interviews? +
Yes! You will receive continuous support from the trainers until you secure a job. This includes resume review, interview preparation, mock interviews, and guidance on handling real interview questions.
Is this training only for manual testers switching to automation? +
This training is perfect for manual testers, freshers, and even experienced automation engineers who want to upgrade from Selenium to Playwright. Anyone planning to switch companies in the coming months will greatly benefit.
